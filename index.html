PITCH

The ancient tree, dating more than fifteen hundred years old, has sustained your people, but ruthless loggers and lumberjacks threaten to destroy it. And with it, destroy the place you grew up in. Guided by The Great Tree, Shizen, you must rally the villagers, complete adventurous quests, and fight against the villains before it’s too late. Gather evidence, heal the land, and sabotage plans to protect your community. With immersive quests, storytelling, and a plan to spread word about our environment, Shizen: A fight for your home challenges you to become the hero your community deserves. The fate of the forest, and consequently your village, is in your hands!

THEME

In Shizen: The fight for your Home, the theme of environmental preservation is introduced through the story of a once-thriving forest-led village now threatened by greedy loggers. Dialogue with villagers, quests from the ancient tree Shizen, and more uncovered in the forest reveal the essential bond between nature and people that everyone must nurture. The player gathers evidence, water plants, sabotages the loggers’ equipment in times of desperation, and rallies townsfolk—each action reinforcing the urgency to protect what should remain untouched by greedy hands. Your choices shape the forest’s future, making the theme of nurturing one's nature both personal and powerful.

MY WHY

I created Shizen because I have always had a deep infatuation with the nature and my envrionment. I have always been one to prioritize the nature around me, doing my best to keep it safe and clean. Through years of seeing how it's taken for granted, and no one seemed to care about about how our planet might one day not be able to recover from how badly it is being treated by humans. Forests are more than just scenery—they’re homes, lifelines, and living beings that deserve to be protected. I wanted to tell a story that reminds people, especially younger players, that nature has value beyond money or resources. That standing up for the world around us—even when it feels like no one else will—is powerful.

Through this game, I hoped to show how even one person, even if they are young and inexperienced, can change the minds of their community, and can inspire them to take better care of their environment. Shizen isn’t just about fighting loggers; it’s about reconnecting with the land, believing in your voice, and remembering that our choices matter. I wanted players to feel like they’re part of something meaningful—something worth protecting.
