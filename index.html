Shizen

"Nature calls—you’re the only one listening."

By Nabila Raisa — Game Developer in the Making

PITCH

The ancient tree, over 1,500 years old, has sustained your people for generations—but ruthless loggers now threaten to destroy it. Guided by the spirit of the tree, Shizen, you must rally villagers, complete quests, sabotage deforestation efforts, and protect your home. This isn’t just a fight for survival—it’s a fight for the soul of your village and the forest it depends on.

THEME

Shizen explores the theme of environmental preservation through storytelling, dialogue, and interactive gameplay. Players uncover the bond between people and nature while collecting evidence, nurturing plants, and confronting destruction. Every action taken reflects the urgency of environmental stewardship and empowers players to become nature’s guardians.

MY WHY

I made Shizen because I care deeply about the world around me. Nature isn’t just pretty—it’s powerful, sacred, and often taken for granted. I wanted to remind people, especially young players, that standing up for our planet is both necessary and impactful. One voice can spark change. One player can inspire a movement.
